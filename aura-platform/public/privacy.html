Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Summary: AURA respects your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our AI financial advisory services. We never sell your data to third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to AURA ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered financial intelligence platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Authentication Data: Google OAuth tokens if you choose to sign in with Google
  • Financial Data: Data you connect via Fi.Money MCP (with your explicit consent)
  • Chat History: Messages you send to our AI assistant

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • IP address and general location

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized financial insights and recommendations
  • To process and respond to your queries through our AI agents
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about updates and support
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Fi.Money MCP Integration

When you connect your Fi.Money account:

  • We access your financial data only with your explicit authorization
  • Data is fetched in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers
  • You can disconnect your Fi.Money account at any time
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transfers

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

AURA is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
